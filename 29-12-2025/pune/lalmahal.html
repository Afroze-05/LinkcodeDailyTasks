Lal Mahal


Lal Mahal is one of the most historically important monuments in Pune, Maharashtra. Originally built in 1630 AD by Shahaji Maharaj, the father of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, Lal Mahal holds great significance as the childhood residence of Shivaji Maharaj. It was here that Shivaji Maharaj spent his early years and learned the values of leadership, courage, and administration that later shaped the Maratha Empire. The fort is especially famous for the historic event where Shivaji Maharaj defeated Shaista Khan, the Mughal general, in a daring nighttime attack. This incident made Lal Mahal a symbol of bravery, intelligence, and strategic warfare.
