Dagdusheth


The Shreemant Dagdusheth Halwai Ganpati Temple is one of the most famous and revered religious landmarks in Pune, Maharashtra. Established over 130 years ago, the temple was founded by Dagdusheth Halwai and his wife Lakshmibai after they lost their only son during a plague epidemic. Seeking peace and strength, they dedicated the temple to Lord Ganesha, who is worshipped as the remover of obstacles. Over time, the temple gained immense popularity due to the faith and devotion of devotees. The idol of Lord Ganesha at Dagdusheth Temple is known for its majestic appearance, adorned with gold ornaments weighing several kilograms.
